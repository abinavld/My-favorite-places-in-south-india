Ranipet

Ranipet is a town in the South Indian state of Tamil Nadu. Ranipet is an industrial hub of Southern Chennai city in southern India. Ranipet district is one of the 38 districts of Tamil Nadu, India, formed by trifurcation of the Vellore district. Ranipet District consists of two Revenue Divisions, Ranipet and Arakkonam, Six Taluks., Arcot, Walajah, Kalavai, Sholinghur, Arakkonam, and Nemili, Eighteen Revenue Firkas, Arakkonam South, Arakonam North, Pallur, Paranji, Kaveripakkam, Panapakkam, Nemili, Sholinghur, Banavaram, Velam, Walajah,Ranipet, Visharam, Arcot, ...
